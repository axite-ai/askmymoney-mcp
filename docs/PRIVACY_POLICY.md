What We Collect
---------------
- Account data from Plaid (accounts, balances, transactions) to provide financial features.
- User-provided info (email, name) for authentication and communication.
- Technical data (logs, device/browser metadata) for security and reliability.

How We Use Data
---------------
- Deliver product features (account linkage, balances, transactions, insights).
- Secure the service (fraud detection, rate limiting, abuse prevention).
- Support and troubleshooting with user consent where needed.

Consent
-------
- We present a consent notice before collecting or processing Plaid-derived data, describing purposes and data types.
- Users may withdraw consent and disconnect institutions at any time; we delete associated tokens and purge data per the Data Retention and Disposal Policy.
